1.4.3 Boolean Logic 5: D-type Flip-Flops

D-type flip-flops are a type of digital storage element that can store one bit of information.
They are commonly used in sequential circuits to store data and are triggered by a clock signal.
In this lesson, we will explore the operation of D-type flip-flops, including their truth tables and timing diagrams.
